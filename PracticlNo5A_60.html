WCE Computer Science and Engineering Department

CSE Department Information

In 1986 the UG program in Computer Science and Engineering was introduced. PG program in CSE was introduced in 1997.
Computer literacy has become essential in the modern world therefore every graduate student is expected to have working knowledge of computer hence our “Latthe Education Society” has decided to start computer science subject for B.Sc. course. Department of Computer Science was established in the year June 2010-2011. The Computer Science is one of the optional subjects of B.Sc. course Every Year more than twenty students were passed B.Sc. These alumni are placed in various fields such as computer firms like cognizant solutions, Infosys, List Software Pvt. Ltd. etc . Many of the our students are get admission to P.G. like M.Sc. Computer science, M.C.A. and M.B.A. etc. The department has well equipped laboratory. The Laboratory is enriched with sophisticated and advanced equipments like Intel i3 Machines, Separate Linux and Windows Server, L.C.D. Projector etc. At present Four Faculty members and two administrative staff are working in the department. The faculty member has attended Various Seminars and Workshops. Department was Organizes workshop on ” Andriod Technology”, VB.Net Technology, Projects etc. Department also Organizes three-four days study tour for B.Sc. III year Students.
